FAQ

Where do my donations go?

Donations go towards the bee's santuaries and our stores.

Are our products organic?

Yes! Our products are made without any additives.

Are all flavors available at every store location?

Flavors can be sent to each store location in 3-5 days, but must be pre-ordered. Select stores only carry their speciality flavors.

Is honey and wax harvesting ethical?

Yes, we take care of our bees in order to help preserve the North Carolina Bee Population.

Where do the bee's come from?

We rescue our bees from various locations where the bees are disturbing the public or endangering themselves.
